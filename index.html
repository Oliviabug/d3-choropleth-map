EU LOBBYING EXPENDITURES BY EUROPEAN COUNTRIES

The data we used to analyse the amount of money spent on EU lobbying by countries have been extracted from the EU transparency register.

Belgium have been removed because it was skewing the all map. Indeed, many companies have their headquarters in Belgium but it doesn't mean that they are Belgian; it means that they want to be closed to the European Parliament.

As Belgium, Belarus is in grey but for a different reason: according to the EU transparency register, this country didn't spend any moeny for EU lobbying this last three years.
